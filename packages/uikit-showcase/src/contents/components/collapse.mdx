Collapse includes two items:
- A button or any way to enable/disable the collapse
- The collapsed content

Three State:
- collapsed: `collapsed`
- Collapsing: `collapsing`
- Show the content: `collapsed show`

<div>
    <button data-hk-target="collapseContentId">Toggler</button>
    <div className="hk1 collapse show" id="collapseContentId">
        Content to toggle
    </div>
</div>
